How to Find Clients on Google

Google is one of the most powerful tools for finding overseas clients, yet many exporters do not know how to use it effectively.

Why Google Is So Powerful

Unlike B2B platforms, Google allows you to directly find companies that are already active and searching for products.

Basic Search Methods

  • Use keywords like "supplier", "importer", "distributor"
  • Add country names to target regions
  • Search specific niches (e.g. furniture importer USA)

Advanced Search Techniques

  • Use site: to search specific websites
  • Use intitle: to find relevant pages
  • Combine keywords for better targeting

Finding Contact Information

After finding potential clients, the next step is to locate emails, phone numbers, or WhatsApp contacts.

Improving Efficiency

Manual searching takes time. Many businesses now use structured tools to speed up lead discovery and improve accuracy.

Conclusion

With the right approach, Google can become a stable and scalable source of overseas clients.
